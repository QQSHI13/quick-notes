# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

## Overview

Quick Notes Extension is a Windows Command Palette extension that creates and manages markdown notes locally on your device.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

Quick Notes Extension:
- ✅ Creates and saves notes locally on your computer
- ✅ Does NOT send any data to external servers
- ✅ Does NOT collect usage analytics
- ✅ Does NOT track your activity
- ✅ Does NOT require an internet connection

## Local Data Storage

All your notes are stored locally in the directory you configure (default: `%USERPROFILE%\Documents\QuickNotes`).
The settings are stored locally in %USERPROFILE%\AppData\Local\QuickNotes

## Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to your notes or any data created by this extension.
